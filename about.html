About the Issue

What is Sargassum?

Sargassum is a floating brown seaweed that supports marine life offshore but causes problems when large amounts wash onto beaches.

Why It Reaches Belize

Climate change, warmer waters, and nutrient runoff increase growth, while ocean currents carry it toward the Caribbean.
